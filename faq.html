Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

1. How do I book a pet sitter?

Booking is easy! Simply search for available pet sitters in your area, choose the one that fits your needs, select your dates and services, and confirm your booking online.



2. What services do pet sitters offer?

Pet sitters may offer services such as:

  1. -Dog walking
  2. -Overnight pet sitting
  3. -Drop-in visits
  4. -Pet feeding and playtime
  5. -Medication administration
  6. -House sitting with pet care
Service availability may vary by sitter.



3. Are your pet sitters experienced and vetted?

Yes. All pet sitters go through a screening process, which may include identity verification, background checks, experience reviews, and client ratings.



4. Can I meet the pet sitter before booking?

Absolutely. Many sitters offer a meet-and-greet (virtual or in person) so you and your pet can get comfortable before the booking begins.



5. What types of pets do sitters care for?

Most sitters care for dogs and cats, and many also look after birds, rabbits, reptiles, and other small animals. Check individual sitter profiles for details.



6. What if my pet has special needs or takes medication?

No problem. You can filter sitters by experience with senior pets, special-needs animals, or medication administration. Be sure to share detailed instructions when booking.



7. What if I notice animal abuse or cruelty? How can I report it?

We hope you never encounter such incidence. However, in the event that such incident happens, please report it to DSPCA using this form.
